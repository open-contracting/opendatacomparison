The datamap applies to the Standing Offers and Supply Arrangements download in the Standing Offers and Supply Arrangements dataset published by Buyandsell.gc.ca. The "End User Entity" field represents government bodies. Many fields cover information in ADD ON and GOODS / SERVICES concepts.
